Students for Justice in Palestine

February Newsletter

Students for Justice in Palestine is a student-run organization at the University of Georgia, that was founded in 2005, and is made up of a diverse group of students, faculty, staff, and community members. SJP seeks to act in accordance with democratic principles in order to promote justice, human rights, and the right of self-determination for the Palestinian people. As students and members of the greater Athens community, we believe it is our duty to promote justice and educate fellow humans specifically about the plight of the Palestinians.

Food of the week

Falafel

Falafel is a deep-fried ball, or a flat or doughnut-shaped patty, made from ground chickpeas, fava beans, or both. Herbs, spices, and onion relatives are commonly added to the dough. Falafel is recognized as a national dish in Palestine. Falafel is served at a variety of restaurants in Palestine, even at your common street food cart in Palestine.
